FAQs

  • What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka? December to March.
  • Do I need a visa? Yes, you can apply online.
  • What is the currency in Sri Lanka? Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR).
  • Is Sri Lanka safe for tourists? Yes, Sri Lanka is generally safe for tourists.
  • What languages are spoken in Sri Lanka? Sinhala, Tamil, and English.
  • What are the must-see attractions in Sri Lanka? Sigiriya, Ella, Galle, Kandy, and Yala National Park.
  • What is the time zone in Sri Lanka? GMT +5:30.
  • Can I use credit cards in Sri Lanka? Yes, credit cards are widely accepted in cities and tourist areas.
  • What type of power outlets are used in Sri Lanka? Types D, M, and G (230V, 50Hz).
  • What is the main mode of transport? Buses, trains, tuk-tuks, and taxis.
  • Are vaccinations required before visiting? Not mandatory, but recommended vaccines include hepatitis A, typhoid, and tetanus.
  • Can I drink tap water in Sri Lanka? It is advised to drink bottled or filtered water.
  • What is the dress code for temples? Modest clothing that covers shoulders and knees.
  • Is tipping customary in Sri Lanka? Yes, tipping is appreciated but not mandatory.
  • How can I get a SIM card in Sri Lanka? SIM cards are available at the airport and local shops.
  • What are the emergency numbers? Police - 119, Ambulance - 1990.
  • What kind of food can I expect? Rice and curry, seafood, hoppers, kottu roti, and tropical fruits.
  • Can I rent a car in Sri Lanka? Yes, but you need an International Driving Permit (IDP).
  • What festivals should I experience? Sinhala and Tamil New Year (April), Vesak (May), and Esala Perahera (July/August).
  • Is bargaining common in Sri Lanka? Yes, especially in markets and with tuk-tuk drivers.
