Sigiriya Rock

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress. It is one of the most visited tourist destinations in Sri Lanka.

Ella

Ella is famous for its hiking trails and beautiful viewpoints, such as Ella Rock and the Nine Arches Bridge.

Galle Fort

Galle Fort, built by the Portuguese in 1588, is a UNESCO World Heritage site located along the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka.

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya is famous for its tea plantations, cool climate, and colonial charm. It’s a must-visit spot for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

Yala National Park

Yala National Park is renowned for its large population of leopards and other wildlife. A safari here offers an unforgettable adventure.

Temple of the Tooth

The Temple of the Tooth in Kandy is one of the most sacred Buddhist pilgrimage sites, revered by millions of devotees worldwide.
